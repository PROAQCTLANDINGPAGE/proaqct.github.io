About

ProAqCT is a one-of-a-kind enterprise software solution for proactive radiation dose and image quality optimization. Our platform provides out-of-the-box tools to easily manage patient doses, perform dose analytics, and optimize patient-specific imaging while meeting all regulatory patient dose monitoring standards. ProAqCT is the only software solution that combines advanced dose monitoring tools with robust automated image quality measurements in a user-friendly interface. By monitoring both image quality and patient dose, ProAqCT truly optimizes patient imaging.

Here's how we deliver optimal imaging

Patient Radiation Dose Monitoring

Users can to set alerts on any dose index at the patient, exam, and series level. Our deep learning tools guide users on how to properly set alert thresholds that lead to corrective action and improved patient outcomes. Using local benchmarks, users can easily compare dose performance between sites and scanners and get notified of any protocol variances. Learn more about our cost effective and complete dose monitoring solution!

Automated Image Quality Monitoring

ProAqCT uses general image quality metrics and advanced image processing such as segmentation, ROI extraction, and feature computation to automatically characterize image quality. Over 30 image quality features, including spatial resolution, contrast, artifacts, and noise texture can be monitored and benchmarked to other scanners. When image quality changes are detected, users are notified and guided on how to improve image quality.

Automated Protocol Mapping

ProAqCT uses NLP machine learning to accurately map protocols to any lexicon. When accuracy of mapping falls below 90%, users are notified. Users can easily remap protocols on the frontend. This powerful mapping technique reduces false positive alerts and improves patient dose monitoring.

Powerful Data Insights

ProAqCTâ€™s data visualization dashboards highlight key insights about current image quality and dose performance. The dashboards have built in radiomic tools to help identify critical changes needed to optimize patient dose. All dashboards can be exported into insightful PDF reports.

Advanced Dosimetry

ProAqCT uses state-of-the-art organ dose library matrices developed by researchers at the NCI. This library includes organ doses from more than 370 computational human phantoms representing pediatrics and adults of both genders and various body habitus. Each phantom consists of more than 100 organs and tissues to provide the most accurate representation of patient dose.

Automated & Rigorous QC

The no-click automated QC tool provides rigorous QC measurements that lead to greater confidence and performance stability of your scanners. This tool reduces the time that techs and other staff spend collecting and analyzing QC and reduces scanner downtime by catching performance issues before they can impact patient scans. Designed to meet all regulatory QC requirements, ProAqCT centralizes QC data in the cloud and provides access to logs from anywhere. ProAqCTTM has all your QC needs covered!
